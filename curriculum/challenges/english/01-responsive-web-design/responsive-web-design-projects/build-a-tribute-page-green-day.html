Green Day

One of the most successful punk rock bands of all time.

Timeline of Green Day's success:

  • 1987 – Formed in East Bay California
  • 1990 – Released first album 39/Smooth
  • 1994 – Released most successful album Dookie which won the band their first Grammy award (Best Alternative Album)
  • 1995 – Released Insomniac which received double platinum certifcation from Recording Industry Association of America
  • 1997 – Released Nimrod with the most successful single "Good Riddance (Time of your life)
  • 2000 – Released Warning which received gold certification from Recording Industry Association of America
  • 2004 – Released American Idiot which won a Grammy award (Best Rock Album) for five commercially successful singles "American Idiot", "Jesus of Suburbia". "Boulevard of Broken Dreams", "Holiday" and "Wake Me Up When September Ends"
  • 2009 – Released 21st Century Breakdwon with hit single "21 Guns"
  • 2016 – Released Revolution Radio
  • 2020 – Latest album Father of All Motherfuckers
